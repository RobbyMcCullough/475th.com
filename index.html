47 5th St. Petaluma

An 1890s era Victorian and our home

47 5th St. is a good example of Gothic Revival Style (c. 1870), with steeply pitched roofs, cross gables and ornately decorated gables. The facing gable has decorated verge board and fish shingles. Notice the horizontal shipboard siding, embellished with a variety of textured surfaces and stick work. Mr. Towne, owner of Towne Drugstore in the 1870s, built the house for his bride.

Source: Historic A St. District
